BIOGRAPHY

TRINITY FAN

Trinity Fan, 17, lives in Bridgewater, New Jersey, and has studied under Tomoko Harada for 10 years. She made her first Carnegie Hall appearance at the age of 8 and has won several prizes since then, including first and second prizes in the New Jersey Teachers Music Association Competition, Suburban Music Study Club Scholarship Competition, Associated Music Teachers League Competition, and Rondo Vanguard Competition, where she was also awarded the Bronze Cash Award. Recently, Trinity was awarded a 2019 YoungArts Merit Winner in Classical Music. In 2016, Trinity was one of the selected 11 alumni to perform in Tanglewood Instituteâ€™s 50th Anniversary Piano Concert. She is also a seven-time Grand Winner in the NLPA International Young Artists Competition and has participated in several masterclasses with artists including Victor Rosenbaum, Sharon Boaz, Igal Kesselman, and Robert Weirich, among others.
